\#\# ⚠️ Epistemic Status Legend

To maintain total transparency, every claim and result in these papers is tagged with a strict epistemic status:

\* \*\*LOCKED:\*\* Core constant mathematically derived and fixed; cannot be modified by downstream papers.  
\* \*\*PROVEN:\*\* A mathematical theorem verified to machine precision.  
\* \*\*DERIVED:\*\* Follows strictly from the Z-Spin action and prior papers with zero free parameters.  
\* \*\*TESTABLE:\*\* A quantitative prediction with an explicit, pre-registered falsification condition.  
\* \*\*OPEN:\*\* A recognized gap or limitation requiring future work. (We do not hide our unresolved problems).
