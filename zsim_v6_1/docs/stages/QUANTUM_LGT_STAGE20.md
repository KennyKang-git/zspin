# QUANTUM_LGT_STAGE20

## Purpose

Stage-20 extends the Stage-19 recompute-aware ledger into a **live sweep expansion**. The emphasis is not a deeper continuum claim, but a tighter orchestration layer that:

1. reruns a broadened Stage-13 preset grid,
2. extracts the best `selected_background` candidate from the live rerun,
3. reconnects that live candidate to the Stage-19 recompute-aware winner and the Stage-18 hybrid reference.

The result is a direct ledger for **live sweep stability** under broadened shape/sign presets.
