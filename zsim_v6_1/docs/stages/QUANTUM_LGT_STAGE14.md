# Quantum LGT Stage 14

Stage-14 extends the Stage-13 broad-vs-lightweight comparison into a preset-sweep stability harness.

## What changes

- Read the Stage-13 broad-compare summary as the reference baseline.
- Lift that baseline into three preset families:
  - `default_broad`
  - `expanded_broad`
  - `larger_shape_sweep`
- Merge each preset's selected background and top control into one direct stability ledger.
- Rank the merged ledger by stability score to stress-test sign/scheme persistence under broader preset coverage.

## Outputs

- `stage14_sweep_rows.csv`
- `stage14_stability_rows.csv`
- `stage14_summary.json`

## Conservative status

This remains a preproduction surrogate. It does not claim exact continuum calorons, production overlap lattices, or final Higgs bilinear closure.
